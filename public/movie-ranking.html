Top 5 Movies I Have Watched in 2023 According to Me

The Best Movies I Have Watched In 2023

Spider-Man:Across the Spider-Verse

4,8/5

The movie was amazing.
From the story to the animation, there was nothing that wasn't great.
The only downside in my opinion was the cliffhanger at the end.

Barbie

4,25/5

I went into this movie with low expectations and was simply blown away.
The cast was great, especially Ryan Gosling as Ken, and so was the plot.
I expected it to be some weird story about a toy that came to life and somehow became
a human, which it was in a way, but it was also much more than that.

Guardians of the Galaxy: Volume 3

4,2/5

As a big fan of Marvel and the MCU, I expected just as much from this movie as from GotG: V1 and GotG: V2 and was not disappointed.
I liked that this part focused more on Rocket Raccoon, as he is someone we knew very little about before
and definitely deserves to be the captain of the GotG after Star-Lord left.

Joker

4/5

This is just in general a good movie.
An origin story deserving of its subject.

Avatar: The Way of Water

3,75/5

A maybe not-so-needed sequel but certainly a great one.
We saw more of the main characters and their beautiful world and how it's evolving.
