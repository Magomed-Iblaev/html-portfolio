The Best Movies I Have Seen In 2023

Top 5 Best Movies I Watched in 2023 According to Me

Spider-Man:Across the Spider-Verse

4,8/5

The Movie was amazing.
From the story to the animation there was nothing that wasnt great.
The only bad part about it was the cliffhanger at the end.

Barbie

4,25/5

I went into this movie with low expectations and was simply blown away.
The cast was great, especialy Ryan Gosling as Ken, and so was the plot.
I expected it to be some weird story about a toy that came to life and somehow became
a human, wich it was in a way, but it also was much more than that.

Guardians of the Galaxy: Volume 3

4,2/5

As a big fan of Marvel and the MCU i expected just as much from this Movie as from GotG:V1 and GotG:V2 and did not get disapointed.
I liked that this Part focused more on Rocket Raccon as he is someone who we knew very little about befor
and deffenetly is someone who deserves to be the captain of the GotG after StarLord left.

Joker

4/5

This just in general a good Movie.
An origin story deserving of its subject.

Avatar: The Way of Water

3,75/5

A maybe not so needed sequel but sertanly a great one.
We saw more of the main characters and their beautiful world and how its evolving.
